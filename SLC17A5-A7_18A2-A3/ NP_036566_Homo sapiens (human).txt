            Summary: This gene encodes a membrane transporter that exports free
            sialic acids that have been cleaved off of cell surface lipids and
            proteins from lysosomes. Mutations in this gene cause sialic acid
            storage diseases, including infantile sialic acid storage disorder
            and and Salla disease, an adult form. [provided by RefSeq, Jul
            2008].
